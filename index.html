About Me

Hi! My name's Brooke (she/her), and I'm a computer science student at Georgia Tech pursuing concentrations in people and devices and a sustainable cities minor. I'll be graduating in May of 2023, and I'm interested in learning more about web development, UI/UX design, and the intersections of sustainability and computing. Click here to view my resume.

I'm the president of the Georgia Tech Veggie Jackets (our club that promotes a plant-based lifestyle) and a volunteer at the Humane League. Additionally, I'll be a head teaching assistant for discrete math at GT (and previously was the web development head TA for a semester and a discrete math regular TA for 3 semesters). I did 3 semesters of Seismic Catalog Declustering research for Georgia Tech's Extreme Events Engineering Vertically Integrated Project under Dr. Jorge Macedo.

During the summer of 2020, I was a UX and software engineering intern for Computer Enterprises, Inc., during the summer of 2021, I was a software engineering intern at Viasat, Inc., and during the summer of 2022, I was a software engineering intern at Convoy!
