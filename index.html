About Me

Hi! My name's Brooke (she/her), and I recently graduated from Georgia Tech with a 4.0 GPA, a B.S. in computer science (with concentrations in people and devices), and a sustainable cities minor. Check out my LinkedIn profile to view the most up-to-date version of my resume and recommendations I've received (requires LinkedIn login).

At Georgia Tech, I was the president of Veggie Jackets (a club for anyone interested in reduced animal product consumption) and also involved in 3 semesters of extreme events civil engineering research (writing code for earthquake classification technique assessment) and did 5 semesters as a College of Computing teaching assistant (twice as a head teaching assistant).

During the summers of 2020, 2021, and 2022 respectively, I had software engineering internships at Computer Enterprises, Inc., Viasat, and Convoy. I returned full-time to Convoy in September, but am open to new roles now!
