Teaching Assistant

I recently completed my third semester working as an undergraduate teaching assistant for discrete math in Georgia Tech's College of Computing. My responsibilities for discrete math included teaching 75 minute weekly recitations for a class of about 50 students, answering student questions after lecture for a class of about 220 students, holding office hours for 3 hours a week, monitoring a student Q&A forum (answering questions), grading and providing feedback for about 25 students' homework a week, and helping proctor and grade exams. I also attended weekly meetings and communicate frequently with the head TA and professors.

Starting in January, I will be the head teaching assistant for a new College of Computing course in web development. My responsibilities will include helping create the new curriculum, creating and grading assignments and exams, handling grade disputes and regrades, and holding office hours.
