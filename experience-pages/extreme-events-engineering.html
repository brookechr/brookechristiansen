Extreme Events Engineering VIP Project

I am going into my second semester of being involved in this Vertically Integrated Project (VIP) under Dr. Jorge Macedo. Last semester, I was tasked with updating/fixing a legacy codebase for performing seismic catalog declustering calculations on a dataset of earthquakes in and around Peru using the Zaliapin and Ben Zion method that had gone untouched for the past 5 years. I knew very little about earthquakes or the formulas associated with classifying them, but was working with someone who had geophysics experience (though not a software development background). With all that said, it was challenging and sometimes frustrating to work with. However, with a lot of patience, Googling, and asking friends for guidance, I was able to make it work and add some new visualization functionality. For this project, I used Python with Matplotlib and NumPy.
