Full-time at Convoy

In just 6 weeks, I ramped up on mobile app development and Postgres, fixed long-standing bugs, and added new features and tests to Convoy's React Native mobile app, which had approximatley 300k users at the time. As a member of the Load Execution team, my goal was to ensure smooth execution of shipments that carriers booked in the app.

I also got to participate in Convoy's Moonshot hackathon week, during which I delivered an Apollo client to Iterable, Figma UI prototypes, and changes to Convoy's mobile app to help users find shipments connecting to those they had already booked or were suggested to book. When piloted, our project eliminated 225 miles driven without loaded cargo per shipment on which the user booked the connecting shipment and increased the batched shipment rate by 18%, ultimately winning the hackathon’s Customer Advisory Board's choice award.
