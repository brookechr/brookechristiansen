Viasat, Inc. Internship

During my internship at Viasat, Inc. during the summer of 2021, I contributed to an intranet application by designing and/or developing 7 features including a navigation bar, profile page, home page, feedback modal that feeds to Slack, and 404 page. I also developed reusable React components for modals, page layouts, dropdown menus, and more.

This project was the biggest I've ever contributed to, and mastering the existing codebase was a rewarding and valuable experience. Though the project wasn’t completed by the time I left, I did get the opportunity to deploy code to production, which was quite exciting!

The most valuable lesson I learned during this internship was to be more confident in my own problem-solving abilities, rather than asking questions that I could figure out the answers to myself. By thinking about problems from different angles or explaining them to others, I was often pleasantly surpised to realize that I could solve them myself!
