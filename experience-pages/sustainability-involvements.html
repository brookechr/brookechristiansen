Sustainability Involvements

Veggie Jackets

I'm currently serving as the director of outreach for the Georgia Tech Veggie Jackets (our plant-based diet/lifestyle club), for which my responsibilities include planning meetings and volunteering events, advertising the club on social media, designing advertising materials, creating and maintaining consistent branding for the club, and reporting student concerns shared on social media to the school's dining providers.

The Humane League

I'm a trained volunteer for the Humane League, which is an organization that advocates for animal cruelty prevention, primarily for chickens and works with Veggie Jackets to provide us with organizing resources and other guidance. My main form of participation is currently completing tasks daily on their Fast Action Network, such as emailing, calling, and filling out contact forms for companies to encourage them to adopt more humane policies.

Students Organizing for Sustainability

I volunteer biweekly with Georgia Tech Students Organizing for Sustainability in their Community Garden helping with weeding, planting, watering, composting, etc.

Work on Climate

Work on Climate is an organization that helps connect people interested in contribution to climate change solutions. During late 2020 and early 2021, I did some pro-bono software development for Work on Climate on their Braintrust Slack bot. I'm currently just a general member, regularly participating in things like climate justice workshops, food waste reduction challenges, networking conversations, etc.

In December 2020, I contributed an blog post called Why Adopt a Plant-Based Diet? to the Pangolin website, an opportunity which I found since the creator of the website is a Work On Climate member.

I also was a software developer and graphic designer for the podcast Conversations with a Wannabe Scientist from Jan. 2021 to June 2021, which I also found since the podcast host is a Work On Climate member.
